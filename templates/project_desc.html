Piping process installation

The project required the design of a complete process installation layout based on P&ID diagrams. The scope of the project included the design of pipe routing, specifying the position of the required instrumentation based on ATEX and pharmaceutical industry standards.
The main software for the design was Autodesk Plant 3D. Autodesk Navisworks was used for revision during the cross-disciplinary meetings.
